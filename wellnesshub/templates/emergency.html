Emergency Guidance

Quick-reference cards for critical moments. Always call emergency services first.

❤️

CPR Steps

1. Check responsiveness. 2. Call help. 3. 30 chest compressions (100-120/min). 4. 2 rescue breaths. Repeat.

💔

Heart Attack

Chew aspirin (if not allergic), sit upright, loosen clothing, call ambulance immediately.

🩹

First Aid - Bleeding

Apply firm pressure with clean cloth, elevate the limb, do not remove embedded objects.

🔥

Burns

Cool under running water 20 min, cover loosely with cling film, do not apply ice or butter.

🌡️

High Fever

Sponge with lukewarm water, hydrate, paracetamol per label. Seek help if >103°F.

🚑
